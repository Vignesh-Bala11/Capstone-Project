In recent years Formula 1 has faced and continues to face harsh critisms that the races are far too predictable. The team decided to put this critism to the test but building multiple models and comparing the results to the official 2019 race results.

2 models were used because the SVM model did a better job predicting who cames first while the Random forest models does a better job of predicting which bin a given racer falls into. Bins are position groups which racers fall into with bin 1 being the top 3 racers, bin 2 being the racers 4,5,6 and so on.
